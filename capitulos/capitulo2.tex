\chapter*{Lilith}
\chapterCiting{What is history but a fable agreed upon?}{Napoleon Bonaparte}
Banished from my homeland, I embarked on a journey that stretched across years, my weary steps guided by unseen angels who diligently tracked my every move. Amidst their celestial presence, I found solace in the words of Gabriel, the chosen messenger of the Most High. It was Gabriel's voice alone that resonated within me, capturing my attention amidst the chaos of my thoughts.

As I traversed the desolate expanse known as the Land of Nod, Gabriel became my steadfast companion, engaging in countless conversations that proved both enlightening and fruitful. It was during these wanderings, in the depths of my grief, that Gabriel shared a glimmer of hope. He revealed to me that my parents, Adam and Eve, mourned the loss of their beloved son, just as I grieved for my own transgressions. The weight of my sorrow was acknowledged, understood by the divine emissary.

In a bittersweet revelation, Gabriel conveyed God's blessing upon me—a new sibling named Seth, who would rise to nourish and populate the land with the descendants of Adam and Eve. This news brought a measure of consolation, for the pain I had inflicted was not in vain. Yet, even in the midst of this newfound hope, a lingering sadness enveloped me like a shroud, for I knew I would never set eyes upon my parents again, nor could I ever fully forgive myself for the unforgivable.

Thus, I walked on, bearing the burden of my actions, forever yearning for redemption and a glimpse of the forgiveness that seemed forever out of reach.

\section*{Michael - The curse of fire}
\begin{center}
	\includegraphics[scale=0.21]{art/images/angels/michael}
	\label{fig:michael - archangel}
\end{center}
As the sun set on a weary day of travel, a profound sense of divine presence enveloped me, signaling the arrival of the Lord Himself. His majestic form materialized before me, clad in resplendent armor that shimmered like silver, leaving his torso exposed. A spear crossed behind a shield, firmly gripped in his left arm, while his countenance bore the wisdom of ages. His white hair flowed like a cascading waterfall, and his eyes, deep as the boundless sea, sparkled with an otherworldly blue hue. His towering stature commanded awe, and his skin seemed to shift between the hues of mortal flesh and the ethereal glow of marble and embers.

Seating ourselves upon a fallen log, the Lord and I engaged in a conversation that transcended time and space. We delved into the mysteries of creation and the depths of my own deeds, as if unraveling the tapestry of my existence. It was a gentle exchange, filled with understanding and compassion. As time ebbed away, the archangel Michael spoke of the Father's boundless mercy, His unwavering desire to extend forgiveness to me.

Yet, in the depths of my being, a tumultuous mix of emotions raged. My soul resonated with the haunting laments of my slain brother, the echoes of his suffering etched upon my heart. Amidst this maelstrom of conflicting feelings, I mustered the strength to utter a resolute "No." It was not a mere act of defiance or a tantrum, but a reflection of the profound struggle within me. Above all, I was burdened by an overwhelming inability to forgive myself, to shed the shackles of my own guilt and find solace in redemption.

As my response reverberated through the ether, Michael, ever perceptive, beheld the depths of my heart. Sadness flickered across his countenance, a shared sorrow for the weight I bore, unable to grasp the forgiveness that lay within my grasp.

\begin{dialogue}
	\speak{Michael}"I share in your anguish, Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve. I bear no delight in the course of action I am bound to undertake."
	\speak{Cain}"Wherefore is the time and mercy of God, O Michael, prince of the heavens? Should I not be granted time to comprehend and ponder upon mine own emotions, O guardian of the people of God? Let thy guidance and protection be extended, that I may find solace in the divine wisdom and discernment bestowed upon thee."
	\speak{Michael}"I, too, cannot pass judgment, for it is the Father who hath bestowed upon me divine instructions to follow. When the Word of the Father speaketh, the Word of the Father cometh to pass, and all shall be done according to His will."	
	\speak{Cain}"Not by His grace, but my own will I live, in pride."
	\speak{Michael}"Verily, I beseech thee to reconsider, for thine heart hath become an open gate unto the abyss of sins, and these transgressions have led thee astray, causing thee to commit deeds of great grievance. Let not the grip of pride hold dominion over thy choices, I entreat thee, for I bear no desire to bestow upon thee the curse of fire as a consequence of thy stubbornness and defiance."
	\speak{Cain}"I stand at the crossroads of two sins, torn between the falsehood of acquiescence to Father's desires and the arrogance of defiance. But it is in this very quandary that Father has placed me. I must remain true to my own essence, and therefore, I must respectfully decline."
	\speak{Michael}"Thus, as long as you roam this land, you and your progeny shall tremble in the presence of my living flame, which shall sear and relish in the taste of your flesh."
\end{dialogue}

 Michael, guided by our Father, used his sweet and profound voice to curse me and I felt suffering in him. He waved goodbye and left my presence flying away.


\section*{Raphael - The curse of sun}
\begin{center}

In the realm between waking and dreaming, I found myself once again on a solitary journey, far removed from the familiar. A second presence graced my path, emanating a radiant aura that cloaked their figure in a shimmering white robe. Their eyes bore the intensity of the sun's raw power, while a cascade of fiery red hair flowed, rivaling even the lofty stature of Michael. Though their voice resonated with the fervor of a blazing furnace, no flames licked at their presence. Together, we embarked on a solemn walk, our strides matching the rhythm of newfound friendship.

It was during our communion that tears welled in my eyes, like dewdrops glistening under a golden dawn, as my companion spoke of Abel's journey beyond the realm of mortal existence. In his infinite love, my dear brother had found the capacity to forgive, his path woven with greater plans crafted by our benevolent Father. Yet, my heart remained ensnared by the chains of self-condemnation, incapable of extending forgiveness to myself. How could I accept absolution from one I had harmed if I could not offer it unto myself? Raphael, perceiving the depths of my innermost being, gazed into my soul with unwavering intensity, and tenderly uttered his words.

\begin{dialogue}
	\speak{Raphael}"Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, I bring forth a significant message from your brother and from Father. Abel, whose heart overflowed with boundless love and compassion, has extended forgiveness for your transgressions. Father, the source of infinite mercy, is willing to accept your sins and grant forgiveness, on the condition that you also forgive yourself. Will you embrace the boundless mercy of the Eternal One and find solace in His infinite grace?"
	\speak{Cain}"Wherefore is the time and mercy of God, O Raphael, herald and manifestation of Father of guidance, protection, and healing? Should I not be granted time to comprehend and ponder upon mine own emotions, O envoy of compassion, healer of ailments and afflictions? Let thy guidance and protection be extended, that I may find solace in the divine wisdom and discernment bestowed upon thee."
	\speak{Rafael}"I am bound to fulfill the Father's commands, even if it means witnessing the suffering and demise of those dear to me. It is my duty to carry out His will and exist eternally. However, I take no pleasure in bestowing upon you the curse of the sun, Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve."	\speak{Cain}"Not by Abel´s forgiveness, but mine own, will be forgive."
	\speak{Raphael}"To grant you solace and prevent myself from wielding the curse upon you, creation of the Eternal, I would willingly change my answer if you, Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, open yourself to seeking forgiveness within your own time and embrace the light of the Ineffable, I shall withhold my hand from carrying out the prescribed course of action. It is not my desire to follow blindly the orders bestowed upon me, but rather to witness your redemption and reconciliation.  Please?"
	\speak{Cain}"I hearken unto your words, Raphael, and I hold deep gratitude for your benevolence and discernment. The gravity of my transgressions has beset me for countless eons, and the notion of obtaining absolution and discovering solace within the radiant embrace of the Ineffable bestows upon me a glimmer of hope. I have been enshrouded by the shadows' embrace for untold eternities, yet should there exist an avenue for redemption, a means by which to heal that which has been rent asunder, I am prepared to seize it, albeit on terms of my own accord. Thus, I elect to stand unwavering in my prior response, Raphael, celestial emissary of the Lord."
	\speak{Michael}"So be it, Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve. As you have chosen this path, know that the sun's radiance shall forever be a threat to you and your progeny, casting its scorching gaze upon you with unyielding fury wherever you seek refuge. I bid you to conceal yourself now, for the dawn approaches, and the Sun ascends to unleash its wrath upon you."
\end{dialogue}

The weight of weariness settled upon my shoulders, and as the first rays of the rising sun painted the horizon, I knew it would be my last sunrise. There was an unquenchable longing to appreciate the beauty of this fleeting moment, but the burden of exhaustion was too great to bear. Seeking refuge, I stumbled upon a hidden sanctuary, a cave nestled within the earth.

With trembling hands, I dug into the soft soil, creating a makeshift shelter to shield myself from the sun's relentless gaze. As the rays grew stronger, casting their golden glow across the land, I sought solace in the cool darkness of the cave. Time seemed to blur, as if it had lost its hold on the world outside, while I remained cocooned in the sanctuary of earth and stone.

Though I knew that the world was still unfolding outside, I chose to embrace this respite from the relentless passage of time. In the depths of the cave, I found a fleeting peace, a sanctuary from the burdens that weighed upon my soul. As the sun soared higher in the sky, its warmth reached out like a comforting hand, yet I remained ensconced, seeking solace within the embrace of the earth.

\section*{Uriel - The curse of blood and darkness}
\begin{center}
	\includegraphics[scale=0.29]{art/images/angels/uriel - archangel}
	\label{fig:uriel - archangel}
\end{center}

Under the shimmering glow of the moonlight, I emerged from the confines of the cave, leaving behind the sanctuary that shielded me from the scorching sun. As I ventured through the desolate fields, a third presence of the Lord enveloped the night. Standing before me was a figure as majestic as his brothers, but distinct in his own ethereal essence. His hair cascaded in brilliant white strands, his eyes emanating a profound depth of blood and darkness. His garments seemed to be woven from the interplay of shadows and light, swirling in a mesmerizing dance around his celestial form.

In this nocturnal encounter, we embarked on a shared journey, walking side by side through the tranquil night. The air was filled with a sense of peace and serenity, as his voice, soft and soothing, resonated with words of comfort and understanding. With each step, he enfolded me in the gentle embrace of his wings, offering solace and companionship in our shared communion.

As friends, we conversed, unburdening the weight of my soul, sharing stories, and unveiling the depths of our hearts. In the presence of this celestial being, time seemed to dissolve, allowing for a profound connection that transcended the boundaries of earthly existence. The moonlit night became a sacred space where secrets were whispered, fears were alleviated, and the vast expanse of the universe became a backdrop for the intimate exchange of thoughts and emotions.

Together, we traversed the night, bathed in the gentle glow of the moon, as a profound sense of peace and understanding settled within my being. In the companionship of this divine presence, I found solace and guidance, a respite from the turmoil that plagued my soul. And in the hushed whispers of our conversation, I discovered a glimmer of hope, a renewed sense of purpose, and a flicker of light amidst the darkness.



\begin{dialogue}
	\speak{Uriel}"Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, I, Uriel, the messenger of The Verb, come bearing tidings of mercy and forgiveness. The Lord has beheld the depths of your actions and comprehends the reasons that led you astray. In His boundless compassion, He extends His benevolence unto you, offering respite from the curses that have plagued your existence. Will you accept this divine gift, embracing the grace of The One and relinquishing the burdens that have weighed upon your soul?"
	\speak{Cain}"By the strength of my own will, and not by the mercy of the Divine, shall I continue my existence. I acknowledge the truth of my being, for I am what I am, and the actions I have undertaken cannot be undone. The course of my life is set, and it shall remain unaltered for eternity."
	\speak{Uriel}"Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, I hear your words and witness your resolve. It is true that each soul must navigate its own path, and the choices we make shape our destiny. If you choose to forge your own path, guided by your own will and accepting the consequences of your actions, then may your journey be marked by self-awareness and growth. May you find peace within the depths of your being, accepting the truth of who you are and the deeds you have committed.  Nevertheless I have to carry out the wishes of our Father.  Will you change your answer?"
	\speak{cain}"Behold, my conviction remains unswayed, and I stand resolute in my decision."
	\speak{Michael}"So it shall be, as decreed upon you and your descendants. Darkness shall be your eternal companion, as you partake solely of the crimson lifeblood. Ashes shall be your sustenance, and your existence shall be one of perpetual un-death. You shall wander in everlasting shadows, and all that you come into contact with shall wither and fade into oblivion. Such is your fate, until the final days come to pass."
\end{dialogue}

Overwhelmed by the weight of my anguish, a single tear escaped my eye and, to my astonishment, it transformed into a tear of blood. Each drop mirrored the pain etched deep within my soul, a tangible manifestation of the inner turmoil that consumed me. Thirst enveloped me, driven by an inexplicable urge to taste the crimson elixir I had unwittingly shed. Filling a sacred graal with the tears of my anguish, I succumbed to a desperate thirst and drank from this haunting chalice, a bitter communion with my own suffering.

In the depths of my despair, I sobbed uncontrollably, my cries echoing through the desolate landscape. I felt myself shrinking, diminished by the weight of the curses that seemed to cling to my very being. In the midst of this torment, I sought solace upon the grassy ground, hoping against hope that this nightmarish reality would fade with the coming dawn.

Seeking a reprieve from the burden of my sins, I immersed myself in the filth of the earth, covering my body in a thick layer of mud and dirt. The stains upon my skin served as a visible reminder of the darkness that now tainted my existence. Seeking refuge from the unforgiving night, I sought shelter in the embrace of a cave, a womb-like sanctuary where I could find respite from the horrors that plagued my dreams.

Within the comforting darkness, I surrendered to sleep, longing for the arrival of a new sunset, as if the rising and setting of the sun could bring about the transformation I so desperately sought. As I closed my weary eyes, I clung to a sliver of hope, praying that this harrowing journey was nothing more than a twisted nightmare from which I would awaken, cleansed and redeemed.

As the new night unfolded, I bore the weight of my torments and burdens, feeling the remnants of my ongoing week still clinging to my weary soul. Yet, in the depths of my despair, a glimmer of hope flickered, for I yearned for God's mercy to grant me release from this earthly existence.

To my astonishment and relief, a presence I cherished above all others manifested before me. It was God's beloved messenger, appearing in the form of a man, standing tall like his celestial brethren. His hair, a cascade of golden strands, framed his face with an aura of divinity. His eyes, radiant pools of forgiveness, gleamed with a honey-colored light, exuding warmth and compassion.

Adorned in a white military tunic intricately braided with strands of shimmering gold, the fabric of his being became a canvas upon which words of our language materialized and dissolved, as if inscribed by an invisible hand. These ethereal characters seemed to orbit his form, delicate strips of paper gracefully adorning his skin.

Overwhelmed by joy, I could not contain my smile as I spoke his name, and in an instant, we embraced. In that tender embrace, I sensed the weight of my burdens lifting, as if his presence alone had the power to soothe my troubled soul. He, too, carried knowledge of the pain I had inflicted upon myself, the self-inflicted wounds that had tormented me relentlessly. Yet, he came bearing a message from our Heavenly Father, for he was the archangelic messenger entrusted with divine revelations.

In this sacred encounter, I listened intently, my heart open to the words he imparted. He spoke of God's boundless love and unwavering forgiveness, assuring me that even in the depths of my darkest moments, there was still hope for redemption. His message held a promise, a glimmer of light piercing through the veil of my despair, beckoning me towards a path of healing and reconciliation.

As we stood there, intertwined in a profound embrace, I felt a renewed sense of purpose and a flicker of faith reignite within me. The archangel's presence reminded me that, despite my transgressions, I was not forsaken. In this moment of divine connection, I found solace, strength, and a flicker of hope that would guide me on the journey towards forgiveness and redemption.



\begin{dialogue}
	\speak{Gabriel}"
	Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, hear this revelation from the heavens above. Know that the boundless mercy of the Father transcends your understanding, for even in this moment, a path of mercy is revealed unto you. It is a road known as Golconda, a path that leads to redemption and restoration. I charge you to spread the word of this road to your children, that they too may have the opportunity to walk in the Light once more, guided by the grace of the Father."
	\speak{Cain}"Gabriel, the celestial messenger of the Eternal, bearing the sacred word of the Verb, I am humbled by your divine nature, transcending the boundaries of the Lord's wisdom and surpassing mortal comprehension. Your limits, if any, elude the gaze of mortal eyes and the flawed hearts of humanity. In the depths of my being, I acknowledge my transgressions, for I have sinned against the divine order. Yet, I seek repentance, yearning to mend my fractured spirit and find solace in the embrace of divine forgiveness.
	
	May our future encounters be filled with the profound joy and serenity that have graced our past interactions. May the benevolence of your grace and the unwavering presence of your celestial essence forever seek me, a wayward soul named Cain. 	May the tapestry of our connection remain vibrant, interwoven with the threads of divine purpose and unyielding compassion. In the depths of my being, I offer my fervent devotion and gratitude to the Father and to you, Gabriel, as I strive to walk the path of righteousness and find salvation in the arms of divine mercy."
	\speak{Gabriel}"Cain, son of Adam, son of Eve, your words resonate with sincerity and a desire for repentance. May our future encounters be filled with delight, and may the grace and presence of the Eternal always guide and watch over you. May your gratitude to the Father and to me, Gabriel, remain steadfast and bring you closer to the divine light."

And so it was. As we continued our walk, Gabriel gradually faded from my side, his presence dissipating into the ethereal realms. Alone once more, I embarked on a journey that spanned thousands of years, traversing the vast expanse of the land that God had created for us.

In my solitary wanderings, I encountered desolate landscapes, barren stretches where no soul stirred. The weight of my past actions pressed upon me, a constant reminder of the irreversible choices I had made. I longed for companionship, for the warmth of human connection, but the desolation seemed to stretch on endlessly.

Days turned into nights, and nights into days, as time lost its grip on my weary existence. Yet, just when despair threatened to consume me, a glimmer of hope materialized on the horizon. A distant figure emerged, their presence breaking the silence that had enveloped me for so long. It was a meeting foretold by destiny, an encounter that would shape the course of my eternal journey.

As I drew closer, the figure became clearer, and my heart swelled with a mix of trepidation and anticipation. For in that solitary figure, I sensed the potential for companionship, for a connection that could bring solace to my restless soul. It was a moment of profound significance, a chance at redemption and healing that I had long yearned for.

The path that led me to this encounter had been fraught with trials and tribulations, but now, as I stood on the precipice of this meeting, I felt a flicker of hope ignite within me. The thousands of years spent in solitude had prepared me for this moment, to appreciate the beauty of human connection, and to embrace the possibilities that lay ahead.

As I moved closer, uncertain of what lay in store, I felt a surge of emotions—hope, anticipation, and a glimmer of joy. The long wait was finally coming to an end, and the dormant ember of my spirit began to glow with renewed purpose. Little did I know that this meeting would mark a turning point, not just in my journey, but in the fate of humanity itself.

For in that solitary figure, I would find not only companionship but also a chance at redemption, forgiveness, and the possibility of a future imbued with love, compassion, and the healing power of grace.
